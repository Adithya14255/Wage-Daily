About WageDaily

WageDaily is a platform dedicated to connecting daily wage workers with employers directly, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair compensation for honest work. Founded in 2023, our mission is to create a transparent ecosystem where labor gets its due recognition and reward.

In many parts of India, daily wage workers often struggle to find consistent employment and fair pay. Meanwhile, employers face challenges in finding reliable workers for their short-term needs. WageDaily bridges this gap by providing a digital platform that connects these two groups directly.

Our Mission

To empower daily wage workers by providing them direct access to employment opportunities, ensuring fair wages, and eliminating exploitation through middlemen.

Our Vision

A world where every worker has access to fair employment opportunities and every employer can find reliable help when needed, creating a harmonious ecosystem of mutual respect and benefit.

How It Works

For Workers: Register on our platform, browse available job opportunities in your area, and connect directly with employers.

For Employers: Post job requirements, specify wages and number of workers needed, and get connected with available workers in your locality.
